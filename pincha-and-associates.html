About the firm

Accounting, tax and corporate law, under one roof.

Pincha and Associates is a proprietorship firm of Chartered Accountants headquartered in Bikaner, Rajasthan, with offices in Jaipur, Jodhpur, Kota, Ganganagar, Surat and Varanasi. The practice serves proprietors, partnership firms, companies and individuals through every stage of the financial year.

The proprietor is qualified as a Chartered Accountant, a Company Secretary and in Law (LLB), which lets the firm view a client's accounting, taxation and corporate-legal questions together rather than in isolation.

The approach is straightforward: understand the client's affairs properly, keep books and filings accurate and timely, and explain the position in plain terms. Confidentiality and professional independence guide every engagement.
